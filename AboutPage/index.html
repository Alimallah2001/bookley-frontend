A platform for Peer-to-Peer book sharing

Our mission is to create a platform that facilitates peer-to-peer book-sharing.

Once done with your books, list them on bookley for a score. Use your score to buy the books you want from bookley and help save a tree.

We are Creating sustainable society, for everyone and forever.

We care for earth, we care for the coming birth !

Our platform ensures a sustainable, eco-friendly, economical, and more connected society

  • Eco Friendly
    Reduce tree cutting by sharing your books with people on bookley
  • Thrifty
    It is cost effective to swap books instead of buying
  • Socially Connective
    Provide a connected society through knowledge spreading
  • Knowledge and Wisdom
    Create a wise and intellectual society by facilitating access to books

We meet 9/17 SDGs

The Sustainable Development Goals or Global Goals are a collection of 17 interlinked global goals designed to be a "shared blueprint for peace and prosperity for people and the planet, now and into the future". The SDGs were set up in 2015 by the United Nations General Assembly and are intended to be achieved by 2030.
